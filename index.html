Why Muskoka?

There is only one Muskoka.

It is a special place where rugged granite outcrops and lonely windswept pines meet the shimmering beauty of freshwater lakes.
Unspoiled natural beauty is what people find here in Muskoka. This place is our home. It is at the heart of what we brew.

Go ahead, taste it.
